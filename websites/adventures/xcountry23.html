Last Supper with my college a cappella group the Chorallaries of MIT. The next morning at 11am I started off. First stop Rhode Island!

Rhode Island may be the smallest state in the US, but it doesn't disappoint when it comes to outdoor adventures. With its beautiful coastline and rolling hills, Rhode Island offers plenty of opportunities for hiking, rock climbing, and other outdoor activities.

Lincoln Woods State Park is one of the most popular climbing areas in Rhode Island. It's located in Lincoln and features a variety of bouldering problems for climbers of all skill levels. The park offers more than 100 routes and problems, ranging from beginner to advanced. The area is open year-round, but the best time to climb is in the spring and fall when the weather is cooler.
